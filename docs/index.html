Error-Guided SFT Data Selection

基于错误诊断的 SFT 数据选择实验脚手架

Error-Guided SFT Data Selection for Small Reasoning Models

本项目研究能否把 base model 的诊断错误转化为数据选择信号，并在相同样本和训练预算下与 matched random baseline 做可审计比较。

This project studies whether base-model diagnostic failures can guide SFT data selection more effectively than a carefully matched random baseline under the same sample and training budget.

研究问题

Research Question

小模型在数值推理中会出现变量绑定、公式选择、尺度处理和时间顺序等错误。本项目先用锁定诊断集识别这些失败，再从独立候选池中选择更针对弱点的 SFT 样本，并与严格匹配的随机选择比较。

Small models often fail through variable-binding mistakes, wrong formulas, scale errors, and temporal-ordering mistakes. The project profiles those failures on a locked diagnostic split, selects targeted SFT examples from a separate candidate pool, and compares them against a matched-random baseline.

当前能说明什么

Current Evidence Boundary

当前结果表仍包含 simulated diagnostic placeholder，只能证明 pipeline、parser、selector 和 audit 能跑通，不能证明 targeted selection 已经提升真实模型。

Current result tables include simulated diagnostic placeholders. They validate the pipeline, parser, selector, and audits, but they do not prove real model improvement.
